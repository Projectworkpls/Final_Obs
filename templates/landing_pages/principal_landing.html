🌟 Why This Program Matters

Every child is constantly learning – not just from books, but from people, experiences, and technology. But who's silently observing this journey and guiding it without bias? That's where Sanjaya – The Observer steps in. Built under the wisdom and supervision of India's Legendary Principals, this program ensures every child is being seen, heard, and supported.

🧭 Your Role as a Principal

As a Principal, you won't directly interact with students—but your guidance shapes the program:

  • Approve & Appoint Observers for learners in your region
  • Review Summaries & Reports sent by Observers
  • Escalate or Intervene if any red flags or unique patterns are noticed
  • Maintain Confidentiality & Quality Control
  • Promote Trust among parents, observers, and your educator network

🔐 How It Works

1️⃣

Observer Training & Verification

Observers are trained and verified by admin under your supervision

2️⃣

Parent Enrollment

Parents subscribe and fill detailed student profiles

3️⃣

Daily Observation Calls

Observers conduct 5-minute daily calls to quietly listen and document

4️⃣

Principal Review

5️⃣

Confidential Support

All insights are confidential and used only to support the learner's journey
